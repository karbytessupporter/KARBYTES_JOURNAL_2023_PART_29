Approximately six hours after karbytes video recorded and then published and archived the two video files of wild turkeys northeast of Lake Chabot, karbytes video recorded a wild caterpillar with ornate features reminiscent of a dragon climbing up the bark of an oak tree. The caterpillar video file has been uploaded to Instagram and GitHub.

(The caterpillar which karbytes made a video recording of was identified as being a member of the species named Western Tusson Moth).
